DATA FILES
================================================================================

3. credit_card_fraud.csv (9.1 MB)
   ├── Purpose: Synthetic dataset of credit card transactions
   ├── Format: CSV with headers
   ├── Rows: 100,000 transactions
   ├── Columns:
   │   ├── transaction_id - Unique identifier (1-100,000)
   │   ├── customer_id - Customer identifier (1000-11000)
   │   ├── timestamp - Transaction datetime
   │   ├── amount - Transaction amount ($)
   │   ├── merchant_category - Type of merchant (8 categories)
   │   ├── distance_from_home - Distance in km
   │   ├── hour_of_transaction - Hour of day (0-23)
   │   ├── day_of_week - Day of week (0-6)
   │   ├── month - Month (1-12)
   │   ├── device_type - Device used (4 types)
   │   └── is_fraud - Target variable (0=legitimate, 1=fraud)
   ├── Class Distribution:
   │   ├── Legitimate: 98,000 (98.00%)
   │   ├── Fraudulent: 2,000 (2.00%)
   │   └── Imbalance ratio: 1:49
   └── File Size: ~9.1 MB (compressed 200KB if needed)
